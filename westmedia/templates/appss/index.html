About

The main competitive advantage of the studio is the skills and experience of employees in the media. In its seven-year-existence, the studio was able to keep the entire staff of sound engineers, dubbing directors and actors, who are the basis for the production of a high quality product in the field of dubbing.

More info

The studio also provides high quality services of translators and editors who are one of the best in their field. This fact allowed the studio to work at full capacity, despite the growing competition in the field of dubbing and subtitling of films and TV series. Particular attention was paid to the possibility of transmitting sound and image over the Internet, which is becoming mandatory for all studios working with the major players in the international market. The creative team has international certificates in the specialties of audio engineer and post-production audio engineer.
